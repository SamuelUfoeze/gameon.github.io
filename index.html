What Is GAME-ON

GAME ON is a unique real estate venture combining sports and entertainment facilities with a social mission. Our business model revolves around the construction and management of AstroTurf sports complexes that provide 24/7 soccer facilities, snooker boards, table tennis, gaming parlour and a bar, as well as an event center. These multi-purpose complexes cater to sports enthusiasts, event planners, and the local community, creating a unique blend of recreation and entertainment.
